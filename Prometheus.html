Project Rocket at UCLA

Contributing to the design and launch of a hybrid rocket for a national competition.

Project Overview

Project Rocket at UCLA is a student-led team with the primary goal of creating a hybrid powered rocket to compete at the Spaceport America Cup. My role is on the electronics sub-team, where we are responsible for the electronic systems that control and monitor the rocket during flight.

Anticipated Outcome & Skills Gained

The goal is a successful launch our rocket to 10,000 feet in altitude and recover it, aiming to win at the Spaceport America Cup. Through this project, I am gaining hands-on experience in PCB design and the complete engineering lifecycle within a large (85 person), multidisciplinary team.
