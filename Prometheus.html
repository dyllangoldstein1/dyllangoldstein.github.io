Project Rocket at UCLA

Contributing to the design and launch of a liquid-propellant rocket for national competitions.

Project Overview

Project Rocket at UCLA is a student-led team dedicated to designing, manufacturing, and launching a liquid-propellant rocket to compete at the highest levels of collegiate rocketry. My role is on the avionics sub-team, where we are responsible for the electronic systems that control and monitor the rocket during flight.

Learning Objectives & Key Challenges

A primary challenge for the avionics team is ensuring the reliability of all electronic systems in a high-vibration, high-G environment. This involves designing custom PCBs, writing fault-tolerant embedded code, and rigorously testing our systems before integration.

Anticipated Outcome & Skills Gained

The goal is a successful launch and recovery of our vehicle. Through this project, I am gaining hands-on experience in high-reliability PCB design, real-time embedded systems programming, and the complete engineering lifecycle within a large, multidisciplinary team.
